Viewpoint Park

Muslim Hands Mirpur, through the MH Climate Action initiative, has transformed View Point Park into a fruitful oasis with a variety of trees yielding juicy oranges, succulent pears, and other delightful fruits. This initiative reflects our commitment to community well-being and environmental stewardship. Thanks to dedicated volunteers, this once barren land now flourishes, showcasing the power of collective effort and care. Feel free to visit and enjoy nature's gifts at View Point Park!
