Viewpoint Park

Embracing Nature's Bounty at View Point Park, Mirpur! 🌳🍇🍎 We are thrilled to share the wonderful news that Muslim Hands Mirpur under MH Climate Action has made a green impact at View Point Park! 🌳🌼 Our dedicated team planted a variety of trees some time back, and now the results are beyond heartwarming. 🌱🌿 The once barren land has transformed into a fruitful oasis, bearing the gifts of nature's goodness – juicy oranges, succulent pears, and an array of other delightful fruits! 🍊🍐🍇 This endeavor reflects our commitment not only to our community's well-being but also to our precious environment. 🌏💚 Through every sapling we planted, we aimed for a greener, healthier future for all.
