MUST Jari Kass Campus

Muslim Hands has held a successful plantation drive of 300 shade trees to MUST Jarikas Campus as part of our commitment to environmental sustainability and community welfare. This drive was graced with the presence of Vice Chancellor and brought together faculty, staff, and students who enthusiastically participated in planting and nurturing these trees. This effort not only enhances our campus but also contributes to mitigating climate change and promoting biodiversity in our region.
