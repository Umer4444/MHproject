Cermony

  • Muslim Hands is working towards nurturing sustainable development and community empowerment. Our organic fruit projects involves planting organic lemon in our gardens.
  • We distribute these fruits to local women, who then create delicious organic homemade pickles. This initiative not only offers a wholesome organic product to our community but also gives these women a vital source of income.
  • By teaching them how to produce and sell their own pickles, we're helping them build sustainable livelihoods. This project embodies our commitment to environmental repairment and women's empowerment, making a real, positive difference in our community.
