Bangrilla Community Hospitall

At Bangrilla Community Hospital, a significant step towards sustainability and community health has been taken with the establishment of a fruit garden initiative. Recently, the hospital initiated the planting of 100 fruit trees, aiming to enhance local nutrition and environmental sustainability. This initiative underscores the hospital's commitment to promoting healthy living and environmental stewardship within the community. By providing a sustainable source of fresh fruits, the hospital not only contributes to improving dietary options but also supports ecological balance and biodiversity conservation.
