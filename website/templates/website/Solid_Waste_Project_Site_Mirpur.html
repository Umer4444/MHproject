Solid Waste Project Site

In collaboration with MDHA, Muslim Hands has established a fruit garden project at the Solid Waste Project Site, featuring 150 fruit-bearing trees including Mulberry and Pine varieties. This initiative aims to enhance local biodiversity, promote sustainable practices, and provide a sustainable source of fresh fruits for the community. This highlights Muslim Hands commitment to environmental conservation as a add on to the Solid Waste Management project.
