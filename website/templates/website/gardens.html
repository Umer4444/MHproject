Homemade Organic Pickle

  • Muslim Hands is working towards nurturing sustainable development and community empowerment. Our organic fruit projects involves planting organic lemon in our gardens.
  • We distribute these fruits to local women, who then create delicious organic homemade pickles. This initiative not only offers a wholesome organic product to our community but also gives these women a vital source of income.
  • By teaching them how to produce and sell their own pickles, we're helping them build sustainable livelihoods. This project embodies our commitment to environmental repairment and women's empowerment, making a real, positive difference in our community.

Lemon Garden

  • Organic lemons boast high levels of antioxidants, promoting overall health and fighting oxidative stress.
  • Grown without synthetic pesticides, organic lemons offer a cleaner alternative for consumers concerned about pesticide residues.
  • With fewer pesticides used, organic lemons minimize chemical exposure, supporting a healthier environment and ecosystem.
  • Organic lemons contain higher levels of essential nutrients like vitamin C, supporting immune function and skin health.

Samar Project

  • Muslim Hands Mirpur, through its 'Samar' plantation campaign, initiated the planting of fruit trees in Mirpur during spring season. The primary objective of this project was to strengthen the fruit economy in the region while simultaneously promoting the development of a cottage industry for fruit products.
  • Organically grown food is rich in nutrients such as Vitamin C, iron, magnesium, and phosphorus, and has less exposure to nitrates and pesticide residues compared to conventionally grown fruits. Over the next 4 to 5 years, as these fruit trees begin to bear fruit, the project aims to provide significant livelihood opportunities to the local community, therefore contributing to sustainable economic growth.
