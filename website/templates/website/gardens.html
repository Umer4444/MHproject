  • Muslim Hands Mirpur, a charitable organization, has taken a proactive step towards promoting sustainable gardening practices by establishing two lemon gardens in Viewpoint Park Mirpur and one in Hilton. These gardens collectively boast more than 200 fully grown organic lemon (lime) plants, meticulously planted and nurtured by the organization itself.
  • The cultivation of these lemons has been entirely fertilizer-free, ensuring that the produce remains both healthy and eco-friendly.
  • In 2019, Muslim Hands Mirpur initiated a project by planting 250 citrus fruit trees. Today, these orchards stand as a testament to our commitment, as they have flourished and yielded a bountiful harvest. These fruits have become a source of livelihood for those in need, providing opportunities for individuals who face economic challenges.
  • The success of this project is evident as the ripe citrus fruits have been sold, and the revenue generated has enabled many to establish and run their own businesses, illustrating the transformative impact of Muslim Hands in the community.
