Haul Road

Our young and energetic interns at Muslim Hands Mirpur AJK conducted a comprehensive assessment of various tree species at the Haul Road location. They demonstrated strong enthusiasm for the noble cause of plantation, recognizing its critical role in combating the challenges of urban pollution. Under the guidance of our experienced staff, they absorbed valuable knowledge like sponges and are now fully prepared to dedicate themselves selflessly to this important endeavor. A dedicated pine tree garden was established at Haul Road, with a total of 50 pine trees planted, symbolizing our commitment to enhancing green spaces and fostering environmental sustainability.
