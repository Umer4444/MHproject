Ghazighura

In a significant effort to enhance local agriculture and support sustainable development, Muslim Hands has contributed 400 fruit plants for the creation of a thriving fruit garden in Ghura Khud and Dera. This initiative aims to boost local food production, provide a sustainable source of nutrition, and foster economic growth within the community. The newly established fruit garden stands as a testament to the positive impact of community-driven projects and the commitment of Muslim Hands to fostering environmental and agricultural sustainability.
